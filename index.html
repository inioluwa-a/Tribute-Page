About The Flame Hashira

Kyojuro Rengoku was a major supporting character in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and a major character in the Mugen Train Arc. He was a Demon Slayer and the Flame Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, known for his burning spirit, extraordinary swordsmanship, and unwavering sense of justice.

Rengoku was a very enthusiastic and optimistic individual who always lived by his personal code of morals and principles that were instilled in him by his mother at a young age. That thorough upbringing shaped him into a brave, compassionate, and selfless warrior, always smiling in the face of danger to protect others.

His fighting style was based on the Flame Breathing technique, which he mastered to perfection.
